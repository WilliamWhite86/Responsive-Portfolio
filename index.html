About Me

The next one to write the great american novel. The one to define our generation. That was me growing up. At least ever since my 6th grade English teacher told me she'd be signing my book some day. I'd re-written part of The Crystal Cave for a book report. It was during my first weird period of binge reading.

I'd go on to get my undergrad in 2009 - an english degree from UMaine Orono.

It didn't pay anything - I wrote sporatically less and less. Any goal or direction never begun. Still... deep down I knew it was really my own fault. My failure. My limitation. Eventually I became bitter. I stopped reading.

I've found my career. I like what I've learned - coding is fun and meaningful.
